Deborah’s Weekly Study Plan

Balancing coursework, sports, and personal time is an art every student learns. Here’s how I try to keep things structured from Monday to Friday:

Day Focus Area Details
Monday Cybersecurity Reading Dive into articles, case studies, and class notes on ethical hacking and network security.
Tuesday Programming Practice Hands-on coding exercises and debugging Java/Android projects.
Wednesday Group Work Meet with classmates for project discussions and collaborative tasks.
Thursday Coursework & Assignments Work on lab exercises, essays, and upcoming submissions.
Friday Sports & Reflection Basketball training, swimming, then light review to wrap up the week.

Beyond the Classroom

University isn’t just lectures and deadlines. It’s the music in your headphones during a study break, the adrenaline of a basketball game, or the late-night talks with friends that shape who you are. These “extras” make the journey unforgettable.

Motivation Corner

“Discipline is choosing between what you want now and what you want most.”

For me, that means saying no to distractions when there’s code to debug, but also saying yes to sports and music when I need to recharge. Balance is everything.
